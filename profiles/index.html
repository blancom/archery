USA Archery Certified: Our Archery Instructors and Coaches are the foundation of Straight to the Point Archery’s programs, camps and the athlete development teams. To insure the highest standards Straight to the Point Archery requires all instructors and coaches to be USA Archery and SafeSport certified. In doing so we will provide you with valuable knowledge and hands-on skills that boosts the quality of your archery education. USA Archery, the National Field Archery Association (NFAA) and the Archery Shooters Association (ASA) jointly recognize all certification levels of our coaching staff.
